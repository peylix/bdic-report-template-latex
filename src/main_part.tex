Here is the main part.

You can put your propositions, theorems and principles.
\begin{proposition}
    Sample proposition.
\end{proposition}

\begin{theorem}
    Sample theorem.
\end{theorem}

\begin{principle}
    Sample principle.
\end{principle}

There are also some predefined boxes with classical colors.
\begin{greenbox}{Green Note}
    Some exquisite prose you've written...
\end{greenbox}

\end{bluebox}
